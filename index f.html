Business Model

Revenue Architecture & Scalability.

Client Commissions

A standard 10-20% commission on all client-to-artist bookings processed through the escrow system, paid for discovery and security.

Artist Subscriptions

Premium models granting artists advanced profile highlighting, EPK generators, analytics, and priority Backstage applications.

Ticketing & Brands

Future revenue streams capturing flat fees on self-organized show ticketing and data-driven corporate brand sponsorships.

Viral Growth & High Retention

Backstage fundamentally changes our valuation. When a band needs a guitarist, they invite a guitarist to join Artistant. The users handle our marketing. Artists log in daily to network, solving the retention problem of standard transactional booking platforms.

Execution Strategy

Scaling by dominating niches.

Phase 1: 0 to 6 Months

Hyper-Local Validation

Targeting 1 primary city and 1 key persona (College cultural fests and local cafes). Hand-onboarding high-quality unmanaged acts and conducting direct B2B sales to committees.

Phase 2: 6 to 12 Months

Solving Leakage & Building the Flywheel

Introduce "Artistant Protection" (replacement guarantees exclusively for on-platform bookings). Open supply to comedians and magicians. Expand demand to corporate HRs for events.

Phase 3: Year 2+

The Operating System

Aggressive Pan-India expansion across all Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities. Roll out direct fan-to-artist ticketing for self-organized shows and connect top-engaged artists with major brands.
